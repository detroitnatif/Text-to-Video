Title: "How to Make Classic Steak Au Poivre - Easy and Delicious Recipe!"

Ingredients:
- 2 beef tenderloin steaks
- Salt
- 2 tbsp black peppercorns
- 2 tbsp butter
- 1/4 cup Cognac
- 1/2 cup heavy cream

Step 1: Start by seasoning your beef tenderloin steaks generously with salt on both sides.
Step 2: Crush the black peppercorns using a mallet or a pan, then press the steaks into the crushed peppercorns to coat both sides evenly.
Step 3: In a hot skillet, melt the butter and sear the steaks for about 4-5 minutes on each side for medium-rare doneness.
Step 4: Remove the steaks from the skillet and set them aside to rest.
Step 5: In the same skillet, carefully pour in the Cognac and flambe by tilting the pan slightly (be cautious with the flame).
Step 6: Once the flames subside, stir in the heavy cream and let the sauce simmer until it thickens slightly.
Step 7: Place the steaks back into the skillet to coat them with the creamy peppercorn sauce.
Step 8: Serve the Steak Au Poivre hot with your favorite side dishes. Enjoy your delicious and flavorful meal!

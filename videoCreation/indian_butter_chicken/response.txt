1) [Background image: Ingredients displayed for the Indian Butter Chicken recipe]

Narrator: "Listed here are all the ingredients you need to prepare the delectable Indian Butter Chicken:
1.5 lbs boneless, skinless chicken thighs, yogurt, lemon juice, chili powder, ground cumin, turmeric, garam masala, coriander, cayenne pepper, fresh ginger, garlic, tomato sauce, heavy cream, unsalted butter, salt, and fresh cilantro."

2) [Background image: Bowl with the combined marinade ingredients]

Narrator: "Combine plain yogurt, lemon juice, chili powder, cumin, turmeric, garam masala, coriander, cayenne pepper, ginger, and garlic in a bowl for the marinade."

3) [Background image: Chicken pieces being added to the marinade]

Narrator: "Add the bite-sized chicken thigh pieces to the marinade, ensuring each piece is thoroughly coated. Refrigerate for optimal flavor."

4) [Background image: Chicken cooking in a pan with melted butter]

Narrator: "Melt unsalted butter in a pan, add the marinated chicken, and cook until no longer pink for juicy textures and vibrant flavors."

5) [Background image: Tomato sauce being poured over the cooked chicken]

Narrator: "Pour tomato sauce over the cooked chicken, stirring well for a rich and aromatic base. Simmer for 20 minutes over low heat to infuse flavors."

6) [Background image: Heavy cream being poured into the simmering chicken]

Narrator: "Stir in heavy cream, allowing it to simmer for an additional 10 minutes to create a creamy and luscious texture."

7) [Background image: Ready Butter Chicken garnished with cilantro]

Narrator: "Season with salt, garnish with fresh cilantro, and serve the piping hot Indian Butter Chicken with rice or naan. Enjoy the exquisite flavors of this homemade delight!"

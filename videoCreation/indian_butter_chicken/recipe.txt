**Ingredients:**
1. 1.5 lbs boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
2. 1 cup plain yogurt
3. 2 tablespoons lemon juice
4. 2 teaspoons chili powder
5. 1 tablespoon ground cumin
6. 1 tablespoon ground turmeric
7. 2 tablespoons garam masala
8. 1 tablespoon ground coriander
9. 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
10. 1 tablespoon grated fresh ginger
11. 4 cloves of garlic, minced
12. 1 can (14 oz) tomato sauce
13. 1 cup heavy cream
14. 1/2 cup unsalted butter
15. Salt to taste
16. Fresh cilantro for garnish

**Steps:**
1. In a bowl, combine yogurt, lemon juice, chili powder, cumin, turmeric, garam masala, coriander, cayenne pepper, ginger, and garlic.
   
2. Add the chicken pieces to the marinade, ensuring each piece is coated. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour, preferably overnight.
   
3. In a large pan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the marinated chicken and cook until no longer pink.
   
4. Pour in the tomato sauce, stirring well. Cook for 20 minutes over low heat.
   
5. Stir in the heavy cream and let it simmer for an additional 10 minutes.
   
6. Season with salt to taste and garnish with fresh cilantro before serving.
   
7. Serve the butter chicken hot with rice or naan. Enjoy your delicious homemade Indian butter chicken!

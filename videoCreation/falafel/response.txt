1) [Background image: A can of chickpeas and fresh parsley being prepared]

Narrator: "Start by combining drained chickpeas, fresh parsley, chopped onion, garlic, cumin, coriander, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper in a food processor. Pulse until finely chopped."

2) [Background image: Bowl of falafel mixture with flour]

Narrator: "Transfer the mixture to a bowl, add flour gradually until a non-sticky ball forms. Adjust flour as needed for the right consistency."

3) [Background image: Frying pan with vegetable oil heating up]

Narrator: "Heat vegetable oil in a pan, shape the falafel mixture into balls or patties while waiting for the oil to heat up."

4) [Background image: Falafel frying in hot oil]

Narrator: "Carefully fry the falafel in batches for 3-5 minutes per side until they turn golden brown and crispy. Avoid overcrowding the pan for even frying."

5) [Background image: Golden brown falafel on a paper towel]

Narrator: "Once fried, remove falafel from oil and place them on a paper towel-lined plate to absorb excess oil."

6) [Background image: Falafel served with pita bread, tahini sauce, and salad]

Narrator: "Finally, serve the hot falafel with pita bread, tahini sauce, salad, and your favorite toppings. Enjoy your delicious homemade falafel!"

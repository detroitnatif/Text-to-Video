**Fajitas Recipe:**

**Ingredients:**
- 1 pound of chicken breast, sliced
- 1 onion, sliced
- 1 red bell pepper, sliced
- 1 green bell pepper, sliced
- 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
- 2 tablespoons of fajita seasoning
- Tortillas
- Optional toppings: sour cream, guacamole, shredded cheese

**Steps:**
1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
2. Add the sliced chicken breast to the skillet and cook until browned and cooked through.
3. Push the chicken to one side of the skillet and add the sliced onions and peppers. Cook until they are tender-crisp.
4. Sprinkle the fajita seasoning over the chicken, onions, and peppers. Stir to combine and let it cook for another couple of minutes.
5. In a separate skillet, warm up the tortillas until they are soft and pliable.
6. Serve the chicken, onions, and peppers mixture on the warm tortillas.
7. Add your favorite toppings like sour cream, guacamole, and shredded cheese.
8. Roll up the tortillas and enjoy your delicious homemade fajitas!

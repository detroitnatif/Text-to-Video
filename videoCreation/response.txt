1) [Background image: Three bacon egg muffins on a white plate with a silver fork]

Narrator: "Preheat the oven to 350 degrees and generously grease a muffin tin with butter to prevent sticking. This simple step ensures easy removal after baking."

2) [Background image: Bacon being cooked in the air fryer]

Narrator: "Cook and crumble the bacon, creating that crispy texture. Cooking it in the air fryer while prepping other ingredients is a time-saving hack."

3) [Background image: Whisked eggs with green onions, cheese, and seasonings]

Narrator: "Whisk together eggs, milk, bacon, green onions, cheese, and seasonings for a flavor-packed mixture. Set it aside for the next step."

4) [Background image: Egg mixture being poured into greased muffin tin]

Narrator: "Fill each muffin cavity 3/4 of the way with the egg mixture. Sprinkle the tops with some more cheddar cheese for that extra gooey goodness."

5) [Background image: Egg muffins in the oven baking]

Narrator: "Bake the muffins for 15-20 minutes until they look set. Watch them puff up while baking, then cool for a few minutes before serving and enjoying!"

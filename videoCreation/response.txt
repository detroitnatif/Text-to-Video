1) [Background image: Three bacon egg muffins on a white plate with a silver fork]

Narrator: "First, preheat the oven to 350 degrees and generously grease a muffin tin with butter. This step ensures your muffins won't stick!"

2) [Background image: Bacon being whisked with eggs in a bowl]

Narrator: "Next, whisk together eggs, milk, bacon, green onions, cheddar cheese, onion powder, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. This savory mixture is the base of your delicious muffins."

3) [Background image: Pouring the egg mixture into the muffin tin]

Narrator: "Carefully pour the egg mixture into each muffin cavity, filling them 3/4 of the way full. Don't forget to sprinkle more cheddar cheese on top for that extra flavor!"

4) [Background image: Freshly baked egg muffins in the muffin tin]

Narrator: "Bake the muffins for 15-20 minutes until they are set and puffed up. Let them cool for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy these flavorful bacon egg muffins!"
